Augmented Reality Bike Map

Role: Graphic Designer

Please excuse the dust. This project will be replaced with a more relevant one in the near future.

Background

The final assignment for a visual thinking design class was to create some sort of augmented reality experience using Adobe Aero to introduce us to the tool and a new medium of interaction design. At the time, I worked part-time at a bike and kayak rental store which printed maps to hand out to customers. The map includes landmarks and places of interest accessible by bike, and we often used them as a visual reference when giving people recommendations for sights or activities.

Solution

I created an augmented reality bike map experience to clearly direct people to the bike path and guide them along the most optimal route including which stops to see along the way using 3D models, scaling animations, and mile markers overlayed on top of the same bike map handed out in paper. Developed using the beta version of Adobe Aero, the experience requires the Aero mobile app and an Adobe Creative Cloud subscription.
