The Healdsburg Crush

Role: Project Manager, UX Designer

Background

The Healdsburg Crush wine festival, hosted by the Boys & Girls Club of Sonoma-Marin, is an event that hopes to raise funds for the non-profit organization while bringing local businesses and community together in the square of Healdsburg, California. The festival website needed a redesign to better represent the event brand, attract donors, sell tickets, and provide helpful information for prosepctive attendees. The site also required more pages added for new activities held at the event including raffles, silent auctions, and volunteering.

Solution

The design of the website upholds the company branding while bringing a feeling of elegance, luxury, and festivites. The new site design hosts more white space between elements, utilization of photo and video media assets for presentation, and a grid layout for consistency across all pages. Finally, the ease of access to information was vital for the final user experience, so a large navigation is used to easily organize event information and make it easier to find it. The website was to be handed off back to the organization after its completion, so we opted to use the Boys & Girls Club existing squarespace account and domain to lessen technical barriers for future website updates.
